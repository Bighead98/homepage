About Me

I am currently a Ph.D student in the integrated master's and doctoral program at the Department of Computer Science, Yonsei University, advised by Prof. In-Kwon Lee. I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science from Yonsei University.

My research interests lie in Virtual Reality, Locomotion, Human-Computer Interaction, and Reinforcement Learning. I am passionate about building immersive virtual reality systems that provide natural and seamless user experiences. Outside of research, I enjoy gourmet food, traveling, and yacht sailing.
